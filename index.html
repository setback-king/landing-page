Popular Natural Landmarks.

Old Faithful: this historic geyser massively erupts multiple times each day.

Yellowstone Plains: numerous bison and other wildlife roam these wide open plains.

Yellowstone River: full of twists and turns, this scenic river stretches far.

Hot Spring: these colorful boiling pools of water emit smelly sulphur fumes.
